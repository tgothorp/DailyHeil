Has the Daily Mail mentioned Hitler today?

How does this work?

Every 10 mins the site will check the Daily Mail RSS feed for any new articles, it will then extract the article text and see if Hitler is mentioned.

Why did you do this?

This originally started as a project to try out some Serverless Computing (This whole website runs on Azure Functions). I know the popular joke is 'Don't mention the war' but for some reason the Daily Mail can't seem to stop mentioning the war (can't imagine why) so I thought I'd create a counter to see just how often the war is mentioned
